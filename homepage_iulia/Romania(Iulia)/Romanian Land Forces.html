Organization

The Romanian Land Forces are organized into various units, including infantry, armored and mechanized brigades, artillery, engineers, air defense, logistics, and other supporting units.

Equipment

The Romanian Land Forces have modernized their equipment over the years, including the acquisition of armored vehicles, infantry weapons, and other military hardware. The inventory includes both domestically produced and imported equipment.

Training

Regular training and exercises are conducted to enhance the skills and readiness of the land forces personnel. Participation in joint exercises with NATO allies and partner nations contributes to interoperability and readiness.

Location

Romania's strategic location in Eastern Europe makes its land forces important for regional stability and security, especially in the context of NATO's eastern flank.

Modernization

Romania has undertaken efforts to modernize its military capabilities, including the land forces, to meet NATO standards and address emerging security challenges.
